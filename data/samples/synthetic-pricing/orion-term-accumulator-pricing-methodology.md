## Document control

- Organization: Orion Mutual Assurance Group
- Version: 1.0
- Effective date: 2026-01-25

## Purpose

This synthetic methodology describes how the product is priced and how the
assumption memo is applied.

## Method

- Project premium streams, account value, claims, expenses, and charges on a
  monthly basis.
- Apply the assumption memo inputs by duration.
- Calculate target profitability on a discounted cash-flow basis.
- Apply a moderate capital and risk margin.

## Sensitivities

- Lapse sensitivity.
- Yield sensitivity.
- Expense sensitivity.

## Model dependencies

- Uses assumptions from the assumption memo.
- Supports the profitability study and the approval memo.
