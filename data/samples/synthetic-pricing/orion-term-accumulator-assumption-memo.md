## Purpose

This synthetic memo defines the assumptions used by the pricing methodology
for the Orion Term Accumulator product.

## Assumptions

- Lapse rates vary by duration and premium pattern.
- Mortality basis starts from a standard table with a small load.
- Maintenance expenses include a fixed per-policy cost and a per-premium
  cost.
- Investment return uses a conservative yield assumption with a modest
  spread margin.

## Experience basis

- Assumptions are calibrated to a fictional competitor benchmark and an
  internal target margin.
- The memo is the evidence source for the pricing methodology's assumption
  inputs.
