# Pricing Profile

Use this profile for internal pricing documents and pricing support material.

## Optional fields

- `product_family`
- `pricing_stage`
- `pricing_method`
- `pricing_assumption_type`
- `assumption_basis`
- `model_component`
- `profitability_metric`
- `sensitivity_driver`
- `target_metric`
- `approval_body`
- `decision_status`
- `pricing_risk`
- `implementation_dependency`

## Guidance

- Keep pricing assumptions distinct from model mechanics.
- Preserve approval or decision context where it exists.
- Do not force actuarial-regulatory terminology into every pricing record.
- Keep the profile portable across teams and model platforms.
